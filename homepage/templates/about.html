To upload a slide request:

1. Make sure your slide is complete and all information on it is correct before uploading

2. Click on 'New Request' from the homepage

3. Fill out the form as accurately as possible

4. Note: the start and end dates are the dates the slide will be on display.

5. Upload file from computer and submit request

6. Montitor your email for updates

All fields are subject to modification before approval.
